About

Music has always been home.

Wherever I've lived, music was the constant. When the world gets too loud, I find comfort in quiet guitar — and that's what I try to create.

Atmospheric, lo-fi, somewhere in the guitarwave space. Each track a diary entry without words.

I hope it finds you when you need it.
